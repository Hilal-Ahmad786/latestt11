ISTANBUL BEYKENT UNIVERSITY

Beykent University

Beykent University was established as a non-profit organization by The Adem Çelik – Beykent Education Foundation and began its educational programs in the 1997 – 1998 academic year. Today it has 10 Faculties, 4 Higher Education Colleges, 2 Institutes, 3 Research Centers, The Continuing Education & Career Center and the Distance Education Center..


Why Beykent University

  • It is one of the most prestigious private universities in Turkey because it is always in progress and improved daily .
  • Beykent University provides its academic programs by the newest and innovative approaches, and it enables the students to get the highest benefit during their studies at the University.
  • 50% of the total number of students at Beykent University almost 15000 of the scholarship students.
  • The University provides its programs in English and Turkish languages and it includes 5 campuses, 8 faculties and 4 institutes.
  • The University also provides the possibility of distance learning.
  • The University provides a precious opportunity for students to participate in scientific research, as there are three scientific research centers at the University.
  • Beykent University is recognized by the European Union and many of Arab states.
